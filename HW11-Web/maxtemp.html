    Lattitude and Max Tempurature

    This graph is fanscinating, but obvious. There is an upside down "U", with a peak at 0 degrees lattitude, which is the equator. The further you get from 0 degrees, the cooler the climate. The curve is skewed, with more observations on the positive side of zero. Since each dot represents a city, this makes sense, as most of the worlds population lives above the equator. India, China, Europe, and North America are all entirely above the equator.
