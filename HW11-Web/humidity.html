    Lattitude and Humidity

    This graph doesn't show a strong relationship like we saw with tempurature but, we can still see a good number of cities with high humidity percentages (near or at 100%) around the equator between -10 and 0 degrees. There also seems to be a cluster of cities with humidity between 60% and 90% at lattitude between 50 and 70 degrees.
